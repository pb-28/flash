Why Choose The Flash Pickleball?

1. Nepal’s Fastest Growing Pickleball Community

The Flash Pickleball has quickly become one of the most vibrant and active pickleball clubs in the country. Our dynamic calendar of training sessions, tournaments, and community events keeps players engaged year-round.

2. Led by Nepal’s No.1 Ranked Players

Our founders, Samir Pokhrel and Subarna Shah, are the top-ranked pickleball players in Nepal — both in singles and doubles. Their deep expertise and international playing experience form the backbone of our training excellence.

3. Proven Professional Coaching

With over 4 years of playing and coaching experience, plus 1.5 years of dedicated professional coaching, we offer training programs built on proper technique, discipline, and player-centered development.

4. International Exposure, Local Impact

Having represented Nepal on global stages, our coaches bring world-class insights to the local court. Players train using standards and strategies practiced by international athletes.

5. Complete Player Development Ecosystem

From beginner-friendly sessions to advanced competitive coaching, we provide a structured pathway for players to grow. Integrated DUPR ranking systems ensure players track progress with accuracy and credibility.

6. A Thriving Environment for All Skill Levels

The Flash is more than a club — it’s a community. Whether you are a first-time player or an aspiring champion, you’ll find a welcoming space to train, connect, and challenge yourself.

7. Strong Partnerships & Trusted Support

Supported by major brands like Selkirk Pickleball, we are actively expanding collaborations with national associations and local organizations to uplift the sport's infrastructure in Nepal.

8. Dedicated to Youth & Grassroots Development

We believe in shaping the future of pickleball by introducing the sport in schools and communities. Our youth programs focus on discipline, fitness, and early talent development.

9. Organizers of Premier Pickleball Events

From monthly tournaments to national and international-level championships, we consistently deliver high-quality events that raise the competitive standard of Nepalese pickleball.

10. A Vision to Establish a National Center of Excellence

With long-term plans for a dedicated training camp, regional hubs, and global athlete pathways, The Flash Pickleball is committed to transforming Nepal into a rising force in the world of pickleball.
